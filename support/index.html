Common questions

How do I sign in?

Enter your email address or mobile number, and we send you a 6-digit code to sign in. There is no password to remember.

How do I connect Apple Health?

On first run, or any time from Settings, you can allow Wellora to read your activity from Apple Health. Wellora only reads the data, it never changes anything in Apple Health.

What does Wellora collect, and is it private?

Your data is yours. We never sell it and we use no advertising or analytics trackers. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I export or delete my data?

Go to Settings, then Manage my data. You can export everything, or delete your account, which erases your data straight away.

Is Wellora medical advice?

No. Wellora supports healthy daily habits. It does not diagnose or treat anything. For health concerns, please see a qualified professional.

Who is Wellora for?

Anyone aged 18 and over who wants a simple, calm way to look after their wellbeing.
