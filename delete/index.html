Delete your account

You can permanently delete your Wellora account and all of its data at any time. Wellora is made by Raga Consulting Pty Ltd in Australia.

Delete it in the app

  1. Open Wellora and go to Settings.
  2. Tap Manage my data.
  3. Tap Delete account, then confirm.

Your account and all of its data are erased straight away. This cannot be undone.

If you cannot open the app

Email hello@wellora.ai from the email address on your account and ask us to delete it. We will remove your account and all of its data within 30 days.

What is deleted

Everything tied to your account is removed: your profile (name, email address and phone number), your Wellness Score history, your daily check-ins (mood, reflections, breathing and connect notes), and any activity, sleep or other health and fitness data you connected from Apple Health or Health Connect. Deletion is immediate, and any backup copies are removed within 30 days.

What is kept

Nothing that can identify you. We may keep anonymous, aggregated statistics that cannot be linked back to you, along with any limited records we are legally required to retain.
